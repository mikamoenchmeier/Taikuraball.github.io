About Matariki

We are thrilled to invite you to a magical evening as we celebrate the Matariki School Ball at Taikura Rudolf Steiner School. Matariki, the Māori New Year, is a time of renewal, reflection, and connection with our community. What better way to honor this tradition than by coming together for a night filled with dancing, laughter, and the enchanting spirit of the stars? We can't wait to see you there!
